Fav. Food3-Pizza:


This food is my all time fav. Whenever I am in a bad mood, I have a pizza which gives me immense pleasure.

Description:
  • Patty
  • Cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Chicken/Beef

Steps:
  1. Spread sauce on rolled pizza dough.
  2. Add cheese and your favorite toppings.
  3. Bake in a hot oven until golden and bubbly.
