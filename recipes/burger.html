Fav. Food-Burger:


This food is my all time fav. Whenever I am in a bad mood, I have a burger which gives me immense pleasure.

Description:
  • Patty
  • Cheese
  • Letus
  • Chicken/Beef

Steps:
  1. Toast the burger buns lightly.
  2. Grill the patty until cooked through.
  3. Add lettuce, cheese, and sauce, then assemble the burger.
